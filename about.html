The Industry Leader That Is Inspired By Your Choice!

Sparkles Salon is an industry leader hair and nails salon that serves the needs of Ugandan and expatriate clients. We Perform unisex African and Caucasian hair cuts.
We treat and plait ladies hair, add extensions and weaves. We perform unisex world class detoxifying pedicures and and manicure. We perform Nails extensions and designs using gels and acrylics. We do facial scrubs and massage at selected outlets.

Our History

We have existed for decades around Kampala. We started as a small beauty salon which at a very faster rate expanded and gave us a chance to have six(6) branches well stocked with very good management structures.
